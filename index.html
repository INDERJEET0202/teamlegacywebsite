ABOUT US - TEAMLEGACY ESPORTS

Team Legacy Esports is a not-for-profit organization with the responsibility to promote , grow and develop the quality , diversity and beauty of Esports . As part of the fabric of our communities and our day-to-day life for now and generations to come . Most importantly to nurture , inspire and protect athletes and the Esports culture .
